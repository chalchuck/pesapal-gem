Let's get the whole "linebreak" thing out of the way. The next paragraph contains two phrases separated by a single newline character:

Roses are red
Violets are blue

  * #Num: [#1][6]
  * User/#Num: [mojombo#1][6]
  * User/Project#Num: [mojombo/god#1][6]
